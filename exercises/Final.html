Reasons the Yankees took number 1

  • My grandparents are from New York and I go see a game every year with my grandpa, dad, and younger brother
  • As I said, I played for our village T-ball Yankees for 3 seasons..maybe 1 season too long but it was some of the most memorable years of my life in sports. Is the reason I am such a big fan of the sport today
  • My mom was always a Yankees fan, and she knew all of the players so I started becoming more familiar with them
  • My uncle gave me my first ever autographed baseball memorbilia , and all of them were signatures of Yankees players.

Reasons the Twins took number 2

  • My dad spent much of his childhood in Iowa, so my dad grew up being a Twins fan
  • We went to our first Twins game this year, and we absolutely love their new stadium( not quite as good as Yankees stadium though)
  • There stadium is super family friendly, and they always really seem to have more of a fans first approach

The Chicago Whitesox

  • They generally try to keep ticket prices as low as possible for families with ticket packages
  • The Whitesox moved up my list becuase they have actually gotten better over the past 2-3 seasons.
  • I did a Whitesox skills camp for a couple of years
  • Their mascot Southpaw is cool and chill asl.
